The SHP was conceived to develop the vision advocated by A Land for All (ALFA), our partner organisation. ALFA is a political initiative whose "two states, one homeland" vision is the starting point of the project, and one of the few popular movements in Palestine-Israel whose leadership and members are drawn from both Palestinian and Israeli societies. 

The two are deliberately structured as independent entities, in close and constant communication, but with distinct roles. This gives the SHP the freedom to explore ideas and approaches that ALFA has not fully considered and may ultimately choose not to endorse. The SHP is not an alternative to ALFA, it is part of a research and policy development effort that includes a growing array of projects at a number of universities, with a view toward giving the vision ALFA has developed the kind of practical detail it will require to become real.
