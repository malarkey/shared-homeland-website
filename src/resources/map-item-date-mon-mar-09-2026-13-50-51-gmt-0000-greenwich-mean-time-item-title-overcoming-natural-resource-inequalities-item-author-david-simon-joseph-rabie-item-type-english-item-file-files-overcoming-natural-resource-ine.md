item-summary: "Underlying – literally – the fractured and conflicted geopolitics
  of Israel-Palestine are the various elements of the shared natural
  environment, namely topography, hydrography, vegetation and natural resources.
  Anthropogenic climate and broader environmental changes are accelerating,
  exacerbating the already substantial variations of precipitation and
  temperature in this semi-arid to arid zone. Ensuring climate resilience and
  sustainability for any and all future reconstruction and development is
  therefore imperative. "
