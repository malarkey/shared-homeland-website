Prior to joining the SHP team, Adi researched the confederation model for Palestine-Israel as part of the ’Palestinian-Israeli Union’ project at the Centre for Arab Progress in London.

Her academic pursuits followed years of work on the ground and active engagement in social justice movements. Adi worked as a facilitator and program manager at the Rape Crisis Centre, and as a content writer and social media manager for various initiatives and NGOs. Adi has been actively involved in initiatives and movements addressing gender-based violence, human rights, refugee rights, and advocating for justice and peace within the Palestinian-Israeli context.
